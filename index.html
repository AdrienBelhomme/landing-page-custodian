Frequently Asked Questions

Still not sure? Check out our FAQ section below or drop us a message.

What does Custodian actually do?
Custodian turns a clear prompt + intent into a custom page that matches your brand, products, and audience in minutes — no more builders, briefs, or endless meetings. Then it reads behavior signals and store data to deliver one clear recommendation: Act / Hold / Wait / Rollback — and guides the next change.
Is Custodian a page builder or an AI chat?
Neither. It’s a decision layer that uses AI to generate (and improve) pages under a clear frame — goal, traffic, hypothesis — instead of a blank canvas or an endless chat.
Do I need analytics setup or a dashboard?
No. Custodian doesn’t add dashboards. It runs its own tracking + decision metrics, combines that with key Shopify data, and returns a Daily Brief you can act on.
How does the Daily Brief work?
The Daily Brief compresses signals into one narrative decision: what changed, what it means, what to do now, and when to reconsider — with a clear action and a clear reason.
What if the recommendation is wrong?
You don’t have to apply the suggestion. And if you do and it’s wrong: you always have rollback. Custodian keeps a decision receipt and version history so you can revert fast and stay in control.
